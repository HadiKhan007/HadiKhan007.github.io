AI Expense Management App

SpendSail

SpendSail is an AI-powered expense management app focused on helping users understand where money goes and how to optimize spending. It combines automatic categorization, clean reporting, and smart summaries in a polished, easy-to-use product experience.

Next.jsReactTypeScriptData VisualizationAI Categorization

Key Highlights

AI-assisted expense categorization
Clear budget and spending breakdowns
Fast transaction browsing
Insight cards for decision making
Mobile-first finance UX

Challenges Solved

Data-dense screen design

Financial dashboards became visually heavy and hard to scan quickly.

Reworked hierarchy with modular cards, tighter spacing rhythm, and prominent insight surfaces.

Category confidence

AI predictions needed user trust and easy correction paths.

Added transparent category cues and quick-edit actions to keep control with the user.

Performance with growing history

Large expense histories risked slower list rendering and filtering.

Applied optimized list rendering and lean filter state handling for smooth interactions.
